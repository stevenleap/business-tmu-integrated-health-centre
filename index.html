About the centre

The TMU Integrated Health Centre is part of Toronto Metropolitan University’s student wellbeing services, offering primary care, mental health support, and wellness programs tailored to the student community. Located at 350 Victoria Street, the centre provides a welcoming space for students to access healthcare on campus.
